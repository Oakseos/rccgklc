Overview

Hebrew 11:35 Women received back their dead raised to life again. This is our prayer outreach. It will go live in January. The initiative was born out of a great desire to support ladies in the place of prayer. More than ever our county need prayer and women have been known to stand firmly in the place of prayer. We are looking forward to hosting a lot of anointed women of God from all over the country in our first edition in January..
