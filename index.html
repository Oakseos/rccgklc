Welcome to

R.C.C.G KINGDOM LIFE CONVOCATION (KLC)

Welcome to the Redeemed Christian Church of God (RCCG) Kingdom Life Convocation Doncaster; a part of RCCG worldwide. We are a multi-ethnic group celebrating the strength in diversity. We strongly believe in Isaiah 60: 3; " Nations shall come to thy light". Our main aim is to be a light in our community. We are in Bawtry Doncaster to spread the love of Christ to all and meet the needs of our community not only spiritually but in every possible way.

Our Mission And Vision

1

To make heaven.




2

To take as many people with us.



3

To accomplish No. 1 above, holiness will be our lifestyle.


4

To accomplish No. 2 and 3 above, we will plant churches within five minutes walking distance in every city and town of developing countries and within five minutes driving distance in every city and town of developed countries.

5

We will pursue these objectives until every Nation in the world is reached for the Lord Jesus Christ

Community Activities

Knowledge Pool

Knowledge Pool is a registered nonprofit social enterprise that offers free career enhancement training and support to our community; Doncaster.
This initiative was borne out of the desire to support young people achieve higher and bridge the knowledge gaps in employer's desirable skills that could help the unemployed get in the job market and offer opportunities for career progression to the employed.

Giftrete

Environmental sustainability program that encourages gift recycling. Giftrete is a website specialized in reducing household waste with over 200,000 registered users worldwide.
Individuals, councils, corporations, agencies and charities benefit of our features to achieve sustainable and measurable waste reduction
