Who are we?

We are a multi-ethnic mission with a mandate of spreading the love of God all over the world. We believe it is possible for men of all colour, language and ethnicity to worship God and serve Him with all their heart. We see God in every work of His hands. We are a praying and evangelical church. We believe in praying for leaders; 1 Tim. 2:2.
The bible says in Jeremiah 29:7 ''Seek the prosperity of the city to which I have sent you''. This is why we not only pray for our cities we engage in charity activities. One of our community engagement platform is ''Knowledge Pool'' a career enhancement platform the offers free IT training and apprenticeship opportunities to the members of our community.

Our History

The Redeemed Christian Church of God; RCCG was established in 1952, at 9 Willoughby Street in Lagos Nigeria by Rev. Josiah Olufemi Akindayomi. The location was later moved to the present site of the Headquarters of the church at 1-5 Redemption Way, Ebute-Metta, Lagos (formerly 1a, Cemetery Street).
The present General Overseer; Pastor Enoch Adejare Adeboye a lecturer of Mathematics at the University of Lagos took over the leadership of the church after the death of the founder in 1980.
The congregation that started with less than 10 people had grown to congregations of millions all over the world with churches spreading over 190 nations of the world. In United Kingdom there are over 850 parishes of the Redeemed Christian Church of God (RCCG). We are proud to be a part of this river; flowing from the heart of Africa to the rest of the world. We have been in Doncaster for over 15 years and recently planted this parish in Bawtry

Mission & Vision



1. To make heaven.



2. To take as many people with us.



3. To have a member of RCCG in every family of all nations.




4. To accomplish No. 1 above, holiness will be our lifestyle.


5. To accomplish No. 2 and 3 above, we will plant churches within five minutes walking distance in every city and town of developing countries and within five minutes driving distance in every city and town of developed countries.



6. We will pursue these objectives until every Nation in the world is reached for the Lord Jesus Christ


Our Belief

Ps. 33:6-9 As revealed unto us by the Bible, we believe that there is only one God, Who is the Creator of both visible and invisible creatures -Gen. 1:1; Ps. 86:9-10; Is. 43:10-11; John 1:1-3. Only God will be in existence forever. Ez. 3:14; in God, every creature receives life -John 5:26.
We believe that the Bible is written by the inspiration of God 2 Timothy 3:16. The bible says in John 3:16 '' For God so love the world that He gave His only begotten son, that whosoever believe on him should not perish but have eternal life. We believe in eternal life and Jesus is the way to eternal life and there is a need to accept him as our personal Lord and saviour for it is written in Romans 3:23 '' for all have sinned and fall short of the glory of God''.We believe that if we believe in Christ and confess our sins God is faithful and just to forgive us of our sins 1 John 1:9.
